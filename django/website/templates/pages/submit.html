Resource Submission

This page allows developers to submit their software for inclusion within the Heliophysics Software Search Interface website. Use the form below to let us know about your tool, including it's name, a brief description, the developers, what categories the resource falls under, and any relevant links, images, or credits that you'd like us to include.

After you submit your resource, a member of our team will be in touch to discuss a plan for including it on the site.

NOTE: All web-based submissions are expected to conform to W3C standards, must pass the W3C Markup and CSS validation services, and must support all major cross-platform browsers (sc. Chrome and Firefox).
