exoVAST: Virtual Exoplanet Software Talks

What is VAST?
The Virtual Astronomy Software Talks (VAST) is a free, monthly virtual seminar series held on the 3rd Wednesday (11am US Eastern time). Each month features two 20-minute talks about astronomy-related software and topics, followed by an open discussion. The goal of these talks is to connect astronomers and physicists to developers that are creating astronomy software and databases.
What is exoVAST?
exoVAST is a new series that is built off of the VAST model but focused on software specific to exoplanet science. It is co-hosted by the VAST and HSSI teams. exoVASTs will be held on the first Wednesday of each month at 11am (US Eastern) unless otherwise noted in the schedule below.
